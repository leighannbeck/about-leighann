Get to Know Leighann

About

Welcome to my about me page!

My name is Leighann, and I am currently working towards pursing a new career as a Full-Stack Web Developer. I have always been interested in web design and development. I am a very creative person and always considered my interest as a hobby rather than a feasible career path. But in the last several years I have realized that isn't the case. I have always wanted to find something I love as a career path, and I may be a little late to the party, but I finally made it to the place I truly believe I am supposed to be.

Hobbies and Interests

Creative Outlets

I have many hobbies that provide a creative outlet and the chance to destress when the pressures of life start to bear down on me more. The primary outlet I have is creative writing. I don't just consider writing to be a good way to relax, but I've also found it to be very therapeutic. I firmly believe that media (such as music, television/movies, books, and other forms) have the ability to reach audiences in a way few other mediums can and that stories have the power to heal in some cases. Because of that belief, I often write about the things that touch me on a personal level most often.

Conventions

One of my favorite things to do is attend fan conventions. The first convention I ever attended was in Las Vegas, NV for the television show, The Vampire Diaries. Since then, I have attended several comic conventions as well as specialized conventions (concentrating on one particular fandom). I enjoy attending panels and hearing from people involved in the media I love so much tell their stories. I also enjoy meeting the actors and sharing with them how their work has impacted me. It can be expensive to attend conventions, so I am not able to go as often as I would like, but that just makes the times I am fortunate enough to attend even more special.
